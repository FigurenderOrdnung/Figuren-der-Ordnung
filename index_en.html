Figures of Order

Theory · Signs · Systems

Figures of Order is an independent theoretical project exploring forms of power, symbolic regimes, and the semiotic and infrastructural conditions of contemporary life. The journal appears irregularly and investigates the shifting architectures through which political, social, and aesthetic orders are produced — and undone.
